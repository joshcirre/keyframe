## Description (4000 characters max)

Keyframe is a professional music performance workstation for live musicians and producers. Organize your songs and sections, host your favorite plugins, split your keyboard, and control everything from your iPad.

**POWERFUL SESSION MANAGEMENT**

Organize your music the way you think about it:

• Songs → Sections: Structure arrangements naturally (Intro, Verse, Chorus, Bridge)
• Instant Recall: Each section remembers channel states, MIDI messages, and scale settings
• Setlists: Order your songs for the gig with notes and pause markers
• Document-based: Save and load complete session files

**PROFESSIONAL MIXER**

A complete audio environment:

• Multi-channel mixer with metering
• Per-channel: 1 instrument + up to 4 insert effects
• Master bus with effects chain and limiter
• Real-time CPU monitoring
• Full plugin state persistence

**PLUGIN HOSTING**

Use your favorite instruments and effects:

• AU and VST3 plugin support
• Automatic plugin discovery
• Plugin window management
• Complete preset saving and restoration

**KEYBOARD ZONE SPLITTING**

Play multiple sounds from one keyboard:

• Define MIDI note ranges per channel
• Optional transposition per zone
• Visual keyboard zone display
• Multiple zones can target the same channel

**INTELLIGENT MIDI PROCESSING**

Built-in tools that would normally require separate plugins:

• Scale Filtering: 13 scale types with Snap or Block mode
• Chord Engine: Diatonic chord generation from single notes
• MIDI Learn: Map any CC to any parameter
• Trigger Mapping: Switch sections via Program Change, CC, or Note

**REMOTE CONTROL**

Control Keyframe from your iPad:

• Automatic network discovery
• iOS app shows all your presets
• One-tap section switching
• Bi-directional volume sync
• No configuration required

**DESIGNED FOR PERFORMANCE**

Clean interface inspired by Teenage Engineering:

• High-contrast for stage visibility
• Dark and light modes
• Minimal distractions
• Keyboard shortcuts for everything

Perfect for live keyboardists and musicians who want their sessions organized and instantly recallable.
